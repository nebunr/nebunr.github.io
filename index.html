Hello! I am Benjamin Estela, a DevOps Engineer with Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Nevada, Reno. My programming interests include Computer Graphics and Human Computer Interaction.

Benjamin Estela

During my time at the University of Nevada, I ran the school's largest student club, Nevada Esports, the school's home for gaming on campus. As club president, I ran many events for hundreds of students to come out and play video games all night as a community. Alongside it, I was the captain of our Overwatch and StarCraft II collegiate teams where we placed within the top 10 schools nationally in both titles. From Fall 2018 to Spring 2019, I coached the University of California, Davis' Overwatch team, as shown on the left. I was also club treasurer of the UNR Game Developers and a student member of Nevada's Assocation of Computing Machinery chapter. In my free time outside of programming and gaming, I am a volunteer at my local church as a youth leader.
